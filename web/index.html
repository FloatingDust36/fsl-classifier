FSL Classifier

Real-time Filipino Sign Language fingerspelling recognition — right in your browser

How it works

✋ Show your hand to the camera and sign any FSL letter
⏱️ Hold the sign steady for 1.5 seconds to add it to your word
🔊 Press Speak to hear your word read aloud

Supported letters

24 static FSL letters — J, Z, and Ñ require motion and are not supported

A B C D E F G H I K L M N O P Q R S T U V W X Y J Z Ñ
